Sentracker (internally also called SentiMeter) is a platform for continuous sentiment measurement inside companies. Employees give feedback anonymously; HR teams receive aggregated, real-time analytics on trends, friction points, and improvements.

Technically the system is built on an **ASP.NET Web API** in **C#**, with an **Angular** application as the frontend. It is deliberately not an AI product: the focus is on clean data modelling, guaranteed anonymity, and a fast, honest feedback loop.

Sentracker was an earlier **Centro Labs** product and is currently available as a demo instance at `demo-app.sentracker.ch`.
