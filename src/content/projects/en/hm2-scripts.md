A small but consistent collection of **Python** scripts that implement core methods from the **Höhere Mathematik 2** course at ZHAW: cubic and natural **splines**, the **Runge-Kutta** method for ordinary differential equations, **Lagrange interpolation**, numeric **differentiation** and quadrature, and position estimation via **LORAN**.

The scripts were produced during exam preparation and serve as executable notes: each method lives in its own annotated script with reproducible examples.
